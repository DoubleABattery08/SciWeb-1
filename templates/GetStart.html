Getting Started


To get started, you will need to create an account. To do this, click the Login button at the top of the page. Once you have logged in, you can join your classes or create new classes, enter your grades, and analyse your grades.


To try out the tools, you can also try out the demo account by Logging in and clicking the use demo account button. This will give you demo data for you to try out the various tools that SciWeb offers
