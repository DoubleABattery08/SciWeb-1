Getting Started with SciWeb


Welcome to SciWeb, your data-driven academic companion designed for BxSci students. Here's everything you can do on our platform:


Account Setup

To begin, create an account by clicking the Login button at the top of the page. You can explore the platform using a demo account to try out our tools with sample data.

Core Features

Grade Management

  • Import your grades directly from Jupiter Ed or enter them manually
  • Download your grades as a CSV file
  • Share your grades via text or social media
  • Keep your grades private with encryption options

Grade Analysis

  • View interactive charts showing your grades over time
  • Analyze score distributions and grade changes
  • Break down performance by class categories
  • Set grade goals and track your progress
  • See personalized compliments based on your recent performance
  • Compare your assignment scores with class distributions

Study Tools

Notebook

  • Organize notes by class and unit
  • Upload images or PDFs of your notes
  • Get AI-generated summaries and practice questions

Evaluate

  • Take practice tests with multiple choice and short response questions
  • Get predicted test grades and detailed feedback
  • Identify your strengths and areas for improvement

Levels

  • Progress through Bloom's Taxonomy levels
  • Get real-time feedback on your answers
  • Customize your learning experience with different prompts

Organization Tools

Classes

  • Join or create class groups
  • Access class information and resources
  • Participate in class discussions

Assignments

  • Track assignments and due dates
  • Discuss assignments with classmates
  • Plan your work schedule

Calendar

  • Organize your schedule with a drag-and-drop interface
  • Plan study blocks and track assignment deadlines
  • Manage your academic goals

Social Features

Messages

  • Chat with classes, friends, and league members
  • Share resources and collaborate

Leagues

  • Join academic competition groups
  • Track shared metrics and view leaderboards
  • Compete with friends while maintaining privacy

Motivation Tools

Goals

  • Set long-term academic goals with AI assistance
  • Track your progress on an interactive timeline
  • Get personalized action plans

Inspire

  • Get personalized motivational content
  • Watch AI-curated inspirational videos

Support

Need help? Our support team is here to assist you. Check out our help section or reach out directly for guidance.
