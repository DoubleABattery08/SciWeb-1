Getting Started with SciWeb


Welcome to SciWeb, your data-driven academic companion designed for BxSci students. Here's how you can make the most of our platform:


Account Setup

To begin, create an account by clicking the Login button at the top of the page. Once logged in, you can join existing classes or create new ones, enter your grades, and analyze your academic performance.

Features Overview

Classes

Collaborate with classmates by joining or creating classes. You can chat, add assignments, and more. Access this feature by navigating to the Classes section.

Assignments

Keep track of your assignments and due dates. Plan your tasks and let your classmates hold you accountable. Visit the Assignments page to get started.

Messages

Communicate with friends, classes, leagues, and study groups. Send messages, share files, and more in the Messages section.

Enter Grades

Import your grades from Jupiter and maintain a record of your academic progress. Head over to Enter Grades to begin.

Grade Analysis

Visualize and analyze your performance to identify areas for improvement. Check out the Analyze Grades page for detailed insights.

Notebook

Capture class materials by snapping pictures or screenshots and add them to your notebook. Access summaries, questions, and more in the Notebook section.

Evaluate

Test your understanding with targeted questions and quizzes. Visit the Evaluate page to get a snapshot of your knowledge.

Friend Requests

Connect with friends to share stats, progress, and more. Manage your connections in the Friend Requests section.

Demo Account

Explore SciWeb's features using a demo account. Simply log in and select the demo account option to access sample data and try out our tools.

Notifications

Enable notifications to stay updated on recent messages, assignments, and more. Ensure your browser supports notifications and follow the prompts to activate them.

Support

If you need assistance, feel free to reach out to our support team or consult the help section for guidance.
