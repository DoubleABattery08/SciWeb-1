SciWeb is Here

Revolutionizing learning through AI-driven analytics, peer collaboration, and personalized learning experiences

Knowledge is Power

SciWeb enables students to understand their academic strengths and weaknesses

Originally, SciWeb was designed purely as an alternative to Jupiter-Ed. With Jupiter, students were just being fed a bunch of numbers without any real meaning or context. SciWeb was designed to give students meaningful feedback on their academic performance. We've since added graphs and charts to help students visualize their progress. We've also enabled students to create and track their own goals.

We believe that these features are essential for students to succeed. SciWeb aims to provide actionable insights to students so that they can build their roadmap to success. AI has also been integrated into this system to analyze trends in the students grades and provide personalized suggestions for where students can improve.

AI is the Future

We've developed AI for personalized learning

Harnessing OpenAI's powerful GPT-4 model, we've created a chatbot that uses class-specific data from students to provide a specific and relevant learning experience. With a variety of modes and metrics, the StudyBot aims not only to quiz students, but to truly understand their learning style and thought process. It's like having a personal tutor in your pocket.

Connections Motivate Innovation

SciWeb creates opportunities for students to connect

SciWeb is more than just a gradebook. It's a platform for students to connect with each other. Just by pulling your grades from Jupiter, SciWeb connects you with other students in your classes. You can add friends, create study groups, and even compete with each other. We believe that by making learning social, we can make it more engaging and fun.

Who we are

SciWeb is an initiative built for Bronx Science students, by Bronx Science students

Membership

We have over 20 active members in our coding, conceptualization, and marketing departments. No experience needed.

What we do
