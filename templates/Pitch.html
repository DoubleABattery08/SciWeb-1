Our Mission

About Us

SciWeb is an innovative club at Bronx Science dedicated
to enhancing the academic experience through a unique,
student-developed web platform. It offers a collaborative
environment where students can improve their grades, gain
valuable coding and web development experience, and participate
in a community-driven project. By focusing on peer collaboration,
cutting-edge AI applications, and a commitment to educational
improvement, SciWeb embodies a new era of learning and community
service at Bronx Science.



Join SciWeb

We're always looking for new members to help design, code, and market
the website! Joining SciWeb offers an unparalleled opportunity to be
at the forefront of academic innovation, providing a platform for
students to collaborate, innovate, and improve their academic journey.
Members gain hands-on experience in tech development, from coding and
web development to AI implementation, all while making a tangible
difference in their community's educational experience. By being part
of SciWeb, you'll not only enhance your own learning and organizational
skills but also help others achieve their academic goals, fostering a
vibrant community of learners and innovators. It's a chance to be part
of something transformative, where your contributions lead to stress
reduction, academic improvement, and a new era of peer collaboration and
support.

To join, fill out our Interest Form, and join our Discord Server!

Our Team

Paul Nieuwerburgh - Co-Founder and President

Paul started working on the code for the SciWeb website in the spring of 2023.
In the fall of 2023 he began pitching SciWeb to BxSci students to gain support
to start SciWeb as a club. Since then, he's overseen the push for SciWeb to expand
in the BxSci community as an initiative. He currently works on coding the website,
organizing meetings, and expanding SciWeb


Aaron Krasinski - Co-Founder and Vice President

Aaron has been part of SciWeb since the fall of 2023 and has been a key part of
our marketing effort in particular, gaining support for SciWeb from an early stage.
He currently heads the conceptualization team, which is responsible for the design
and function of the website.


Pramit Acharjee - Co-Founder and Head of Marketing

Pramit joined SciWeb in 2023 quickly turned SciWeb from a small-scale operation
into a thriving initative with almost 20 active members. He currently manages the
marketing department, which is resposible for promoting SciWeb on Social Media and
elsewhere.
